About Capital Homelands

Your trusted partner for premium property listings in Nairobi since 2025.

Our Mission

Capital Homelands was founded to simplify property search in Nairobi by connecting buyers, renters, and investors directly with verified owners — eliminating agents, hidden fees, and misinformation.

Based in Ruai, we specialize in premium listings across Westlands, Karen, Parklands, Lavington, and beyond — offering transparency, speed, and personalized service.
